A Little About Me

Academic Background

I started my academic journey in the social sciences and graduated from the University of Florida and then Florida State University studying Social Psychology, specifically Learning and Cognition. This field emphasis how humans think and learn under various different environments and how those environments affect the individual's ability to learn/think/perform. As I progressed more through this journey, I got more into research, as is common in the social sciences. I fell in love with metrics (I had always liked math) and qualitative research methods. The ability to provide a tangible, numeric representation of performance or difference was very appealing to me.

Starting Out

After finishing my studies I set out to start my own sports analytics company. On my journey of creating and collecting data, I encountered many hurdles each requiring me to delve deeper into the technology realm. From Excel spreadsheets and paper collection software, to a mobile phone app and a fully coded website and connected database. This happened before I knew it and I loved every step of the way. Eventually after some great mentorship at TDI I was able to use my platform to acquire a position with one of the larger organizations in the wrestling community.
